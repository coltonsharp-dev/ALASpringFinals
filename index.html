02Printing

The fastest path

  1. Hit the colored Print this exam button on the class card above. The exam PDF loads in a hidden frame and the print dialog opens automatically.
  2. Set Copies to your class roster count, choose your school printer, click Print.
  3. If the dialog doesn't open in your browser (Safari can be picky), use the Exam · PDF link instead — that opens the PDF in a new tab where you can hit ⌘P / Ctrl P.

Recommended print settings

  • Paper: Letter · 8.5″ × 11″ · white · 20 lb copy paper.
  • Sides: Double-sided is fine for every exam. World History is a write-in booklet — keep it stapled.
  • Color vs. B/W: Black & white is fine for all exams. The answer keys use color highlighting but print legibly in grayscale.
  • Scaling: "Fit to printable area" or 100% — do not enable shrink-to-fit.
  • Page range: Print the full PDF unless you only need a section.

03How to Administer

Before the exam

  • Verify each student has a charged device with internet, or hand out the printed PDF version.
  • Confirm class-specific tools per the teacher's policy (calculator, periodic table, scratch paper).
  • Open the answer key in a separate tab on the proctor device only — never project.
  • Post the start time and end time visibly on the board.
  • Remind students to silence and stow phones, smartwatches, and earbuds.

During the exam

  • Maintain silent testing conditions. No collaboration of any kind.
  • Clarifying questions about wording are fine; questions about content are not.
  • Bathroom one student at a time — log the time out and the time back.
  • Students who finish early must remain seated and review their work; no phones or other materials.

After the exam

  • Confirm every student has submitted via the method in the table below.
  • Collect scratch paper and shred per school policy.
  • Close every open browser tab on the proctor device and lock the screen.
  • Return printed exams to the teacher's mailbox or designated drop point.
